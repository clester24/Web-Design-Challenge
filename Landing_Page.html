    • Summary: Latitude vs. X

    The purpose of this data is to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To do this 500 cities were randomly pulled from around the world and formed into a data set.

    We used Matplotlib to plot the data. We focused on factors such as Temperature, Cloudiness, Wind Speed, and Humidity. The site shows the visuals of the data sets.
